ABOUT US

Our Story

Over the years, our cafe has captured the hearts and taste buds of not only Canadians, but people all over the world, with classics like our
Original Blend coffee and the signature Iced Capp®, along with a variety of inspired donuts including our ever popular Timbits®

In 2011 the iconic Canadian brand made its way to the Middle East, opening the first location in Dubai.

Guests can now enjoy our premium coffees, delicious baked goods, made to order sandwiches and wraps, and many other beverages and food
products at locations throughout the United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait and Bahrain. We can’t wait to meet you!
